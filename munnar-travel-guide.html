1. The Tea Estates

No trip to Munnar is complete without getting lost in the vast tea plantations. Visit the Tata Tea Museum to learn about the history of tea processing in the region, or simply take a walk through the estates of Lock Heart or Kolukkumalai (the world's highest organic tea plantation).

Breathtaking views from the highest points of Munnar.

2. Eravikulam National Park

Home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr, this park offers breathtaking views of the Anamudi peak. If you're lucky enough to visit during the bloom of the Neelakurinji (which happens once every 12 years), the hills turn a magical shade of blue.

Explore Munnar in Motion

3. Mattupetty Dam & Echo Point

A perfect spot for a picnic or a boat ride. At Echo Point, as the name suggests, you can hear your voice reverberate through the mountains. It's a favorite for families and couples alike.
